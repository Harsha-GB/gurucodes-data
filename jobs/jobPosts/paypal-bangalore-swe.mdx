## About the Job

### The Company

PayPal has been transforming commerce worldwide for over 25 years, offering innovative solutions that make moving money, shopping, and selling simple, personalized, and secure. Operating in about 200 markets, PayPal empowers businesses and consumers to thrive in the global economy.

PayPal connects hundreds of millions of merchants and consumers through a global two-sided network. We not only facilitate online and in-person transactions but also provide our own payment solutions. This allows merchants to complete payments securely and gives customers flexibility to use various funding sources, including bank accounts, PayPal or Venmo balances, credit cards, cryptocurrencies, and more.

We also support seamless money transfers between friends and family via PayPal, Venmo, and Xoom. Merchants benefit from end-to-end payment solutions, instant payouts, and risk management, enabling cross-border transactions with ease.

Our core values of Inclusion, Innovation, Collaboration, and Wellness guide how we operate, ensuring we work as one global team focused on our customers while taking care of each other and our communities.

### Job Description:

#### Meet Our Team:

Our Software Engineers develop high-quality products that impact millions globally. We tackle complex technical challenges in connected payments across PayPal, Braintree, Venmo, and other business units. We’re seeking talented engineers with a passion for innovation and a deep understanding of functionality, performance, reliability, and scalability.

#### Your Impact:

You’ll work alongside top engineering talent, contributing to various areas like development, design, testing, and more. As a core team member, you'll estimate efforts, design solutions, implement and test changes, push to production, and handle production issues as they arise. Collaboration and curiosity are key as we build new experiences and enhance existing ones at a global scale.

#### Your Day-to-Day:

Develop high-volume, scalable software (front-end and/or back-end) using technologies like React, Node.js, Java, SQL, and REST.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams in design, product, and other business units.
What You Need to Bring:

Solid experience building, breaking, and rebuilding software applications.
Proficiency in web technologies (HTTP, HTML/DOM, JavaScript, CSS, AJAX).
Familiarity with Node.js, Java, C++, or Python.
Understanding of Web Services, SOA, and REST APIs.
A desire to continually learn, grow, and explore new technologies.
University Graduate Position Requirements:
This position is for university graduates. You must be graduating in 2024 with a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science or a related field from an accredited institution.
